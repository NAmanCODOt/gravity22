Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 5, 2026

1. Intelligence Gathering (Data Collection)

At gRaviTy Research, we approach data the same way we approach markets—with strict logic and absolute security. We collect only what is necessary to optimize your experience within our Market Intelligence System. This includes:

  • Technical Telemetry: IP addresses, browser specifications, and hardware identifiers to ensure optimal system performance.
  • Interaction Data: Behavioral tracking within our calculators, clocks, and research portals to improve user interface efficiency.
  • Communication Logs: Information you voluntarily provide when utilizing our contact systems.

2. System Utilization (How We Use Data)

Your data is processed strictly for systemic improvement and operational integrity. We utilize collected information to:

  • Maintain, deliver, and enhance our analytical tools and research portals.
  • Monitor systemic health and prevent unauthorized access or system abuse.
  • Respond accurately and securely to your inquiries and support requests.

3. Security Protocols

We treat your privacy with the same rigor applied to our market research. Advanced encryption, secure data routing, and stringent access protocols are permanently active. Data is housed in heavily monitored environments, and access is restricted exclusively to cleared personnel required for systemic maintenance.

4. Third-Party Integrations

Our platform incorporates highly vetted third-party modules (e.g., TradingView charts, Umami analytics). While these modules function within our ecosystem, they operate under their own strict data policies. We do not sell, rent, or recklessly distribute your data to external entities.

5. Your System Access Rights

You maintain ultimate sovereignty over your data profile. You reserve the right to request access to the data we have gathered, request corrections to systemic inaccuracies, or petition for total data erasure from our servers, subject to governing legal limitations.

6. Contact Protocol

If you require clarification on our data handling logic or wish to exercise your rights, initiate contact via our Contact Us interface.
